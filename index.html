Play Pong!

Classic Computergame

Take a walk down memorylane with this classical Computergame. Or if you are younger; discover one of the simplestand yet surprisingly amusing games from way back in the 1970`s. It really is quite fun and it's also free. So go ahead and give it a try!

Score Player 1 Score Player 2

Gameplay

Player 1 is controlled by keys "w" and "s" and Player 2 is controlled by keys "i" and "k". The goal is to get more points than the other, before the game is over. First player to hit a score of 10 wins.
